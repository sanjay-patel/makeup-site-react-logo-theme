## Local Directory Listings

### Why Directory Listings Matter

Local directory listings significantly boost local SEO and traffic by:

- **NAP Citations:** Google verifies business consistency across sources (25% of local ranking)
- **Backlinks:** Each quality directory = quality backlink (10% of ranking)
- **Direct Traffic:** Platforms like WedMeGood drive actual bookings
- **Trust Signals:** Multiple listings = higher credibility

### SEO Impact Breakdown

- Google Business Profile: 25% of ranking
- Citations (directories): 15% of ranking
- Reviews: 15% of ranking
- On-page SEO: 20% of ranking
- Links: 10% of ranking
- Behavioral signals: 10% of ranking
- Social: 5% of ranking

## Blog Content Strategy

### Blog Framework Recommendation

**Recommended:** Contentlayer with Next.js App Router

**Why:**
- Built for Next.js 14 App Router
- Write in MDX (Markdown + React)
- Type-safe with TypeScript
- Reuse existing components (FadeIn, StaggerContainer)
- Zero config for basic setup

**Alternative Options:**
- Next-MDX-Remote (lighter weight)
- Headless CMS (Sanity/Contentful) for non-technical authors

### Blog Structure

```
/app/blog/
  - page.tsx                    # Blog listing page
  - [slug]/page.tsx             # Individual blog post

/content/blog/
  - bridal-makeup-checklist.mdx
  - destination-wedding-tips.mdx
  - premium-brands-review.mdx

/components/blog/
  - BlogCard.tsx                # Preview card for blog list
  - BlogContent.tsx             # MDX content wrapper
  - ShareButtons.tsx            # Social sharing
```

## Notes & Important Reminders

1. **NAP Consistency is Critical**
   - Use EXACT same business name everywhere
   - Use EXACT same address format everywhere
   - Use EXACT same phone number everywhere
   - Inconsistency hurts rankings

2. **Reviews are Gold**
   - Ask every happy client for review
   - Make it easy (send direct Google link)
   - Respond to ALL reviews within 24 hours
   - Never buy fake reviews (Google penalty)

3. **Photos Matter**
   - Upload HIGH QUALITY photos only
   - Show your work, products, studio
   - Update monthly with fresh content
   - Before/after photos perform best

4. **Content is King**
   - Post regularly to Google Business
   - Keep Instagram active (3-4x week minimum)
   - Blog posts compound over time
   - Video content gets priority in search

5. **Patience Required**
   - SEO takes 3-6 months to show results
   - Consistency is more important than perfection
   - Keep doing the work even if results are slow
   - Track progress monthly to stay motivated
