## Photography Tips for Product Page

### Setup:
- Clean white background or marble surface
- Natural lighting (window light) or ring light
- Group products by brand
- Include packaging (boxes/bottles) for authenticity

### What to Photograph:
1. **Hero Shot:** All products together (collection overview)
2. **Per Brand:** 3-5 key products from each brand
3. **Details:** Product labels visible (prove authenticity)
4. **In Use:** Optional - products on vanity/in use

### Image Specs:
- Size: 1200x800px minimum
- Format: JPG (compressed) or WebP
- Alt text: "Charlotte Tilbury Pillow Talk lipstick - Premium bridal makeup product Surat"

## Writing Template for Each Brand

### Structure (150 words per brand):

**Paragraph 1 (50 words):** Why you chose this brand
```
"[Brand] is my go-to for [specific occasion] because [unique benefit].
As a professional makeup artist in Surat, I've tested countless products,
and [Brand] consistently delivers [specific result] for my clients."
```

**Paragraph 2 (60 words):** Key products & their benefits
```
"My must-have products from [Brand] include:
- [Product 1]: Perfect for [specific use case]
- [Product 2]: Ideal for [specific benefit]
- [Product 3]: Essential for [specific result]

These products work beautifully with Indian skin tones and withstand
Surat's climate, lasting 12+ hours through ceremonies and receptions."
```

**Paragraph 3 (40 words):** When you use it & results
```
"I use [Brand] products for [bridal/party/photoshoot] makeup when clients
want [specific look/finish]. The results speak for themselves - flawless,
long-lasting makeup that photographs beautifully and makes my brides feel
absolutely stunning."
```

## Questions to Consider Before Starting

1. **Do you have time to photograph products?**
   - Yes → Start this week
   - No → Hire photographer or use phone + natural light

2. **Are all products currently in your kit?**
   - Yes → Great, show everything
   - No → Show what you have, add others later

3. **Do you want pricing visible?**
   - Yes → Add "Investment: ₹₹₹₹" indicator
   - No → Keep focus on quality and results

4. **Will you update regularly?**
   - Yes → Add "Last Updated: [Date]" to page
   - No → Make it evergreen (timeless content)
